Sensalite offers an excellent AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) that undertakes all types of servicing and maintenance of your AC to ensure that they are in good running condition.

❰ AMC Benefits ❱

01

AC will be checked initially before service, for Temperature, Amperage and Noise. AC unit will be pulled out, but not brought down. Later physical inspection of the unit will be undertaken.

02

Air-filter will be cleaned. Evaporator coil will be checked and cleaned. Following this, Condenser Coil will be checked.

03

Electrical control panel will be checked and cleaned. i.e. check-up of Selector switch. Thermostat P.T.C.R. Oscillation Motor, Vent Levers and Exhaust Levers, if necessary.

04

All Electrical components will be inspected visually for any damages. Wiring and fastening clips will be checked and if necessary, it will de replaced. Fan Motors will be checked and lubrication of the fan motor will be done.

05

Compressor will be thoroughly checked. Unit will be cleaned from any dust and slime. Stabilizer, tripper Switch, Electrical Power Point for the A/C. unit will be checked.

06

After service, front panel will be cleaned and checked for any defect. Following this the AC unit will be given a test-run to check its working temperature and working efficiency after-service.

Note :-


01. Detailed report of AC will be made and handed over for reference. Any repairs required will be informed immediately.


02. Servicing will be done on Monthly/Alternate Monthly/Quarterly basis as per customer's desire. Any kind of breakdown walls can be attended to with immediate effect during normal working hours.
