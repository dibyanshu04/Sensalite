Sensalite has been established in the year 2010 and set in 1351, Windsor Pl San Jacinto, California(USA) - 92583.


We have 12 years of rich experience in the Sensor industry as we are providing different types of Temperature, Humidity, Air and Gas, monitoring and many other Sensing products for the Commercial, Offices, Hospitals, Hotels, Malls, Educational Institutions, and so on. We have a design group, sales group, IT group, and service dealers for various brands of Sensors and services in USA and as well as all over South India. Our primary hypothesis is to Plan, Design, Execute, and Service. We have countless customers who are happy with our products and administrations.

Our Strengths

Our main strength is that we remain customer-centric with the ability to understand what is important to them. We also make sure at all times to maintain sufficient product stock levels that in turn will ensure that no customer will be left disappointed.

Our Relationship

We cement our relationship with our customers by extending quality support in various ways from product selection to logistical support and excellent quality service by a passionate customer-support team.


Our Future

Looking to the future, we intend to maintain the quality and diversity of our product collection while continuing to build on strong and loyal customer relationships, catering to their requirements, now and in the future.
